Kinuvy — Your Pet’s Life. One Timeline.

Everything in one place, organized automatically.

Kinuvy helps you keep track of your pet’s daily life in one simple, organized timeline.

Log food, medications, symptoms, and activities in seconds. Everything is automatically organized so you can quickly understand your pet’s history and spot changes over time.

Designed for simplicity and speed, Kinuvy makes it easy to stay consistent—so you can focus on caring for your pet, not managing complicated tools.

Features

  • Track food, medications, symptoms, and more
  • Clean timeline view of all events
  • Fast, frictionless logging
  • Support for multiple pets
  • Share summaries when needed
