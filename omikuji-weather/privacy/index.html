Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

Omikuji Weather respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Omikuji Weather is designed to minimize data collection.

The app may access location and weather information so it can generate your daily forecast-based fortune. This information is used only to provide the app's experience.

Omikuji Weather does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information.

Analytics

Omikuji Weather does not use analytics or tracking tools.

Data Storage

Any saved app data, such as your daily fortune history or app preferences, is stored locally on your device unless stated otherwise in a future update.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your Control

You can stop sharing location with the app at any time in your device settings, and you can remove local app data by deleting the app.

AI and Interpretation

Omikuji Weather may use on-device processing to generate short weather-based interpretations. These interpretations are for informational and entertainment purposes.

Changes
