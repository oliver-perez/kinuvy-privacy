Omikuji Weather

A daily weather fortune for a calmer start.

Omikuji Weather transforms real-world weather into a short, poetic daily fortune inspired by the Japanese omikuji tradition.

Instead of overwhelming you with data, it offers a more reflective ritual: open the app, receive the day's fortune, and see the weather through a calmer, more meaningful lens.

What to Expect

  • A daily fortune shaped by real weather conditions
  • Short, calm interpretations instead of noisy forecasts
  • A minimal experience designed for daily ritual
  • Privacy-first, on-device design
