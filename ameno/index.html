Daily Prayer App

Ameno

Put prayer first, every day.

Ameno is a daily prayer companion built around a simple idea: when the scroll grows louder than prayer, the quiet practice you wanted slips away. Ameno makes prayer the first thing — and gently keeps distractions at bay until it’s done.

Schedule the times you want to pray. When a scheduled time arrives, Ameno can pause the apps you’ve chosen to set aside, opening a calm space for prayer. When you’re done, your day continues as usual.

What to Expect

  • Schedule prayer times that fit your day
  • Pause selected apps during prayer using Apple’s Screen Time
  • A daily rhythm of moods, prayers, and reflection
  • Private by design — your prayer history stays in your own iCloud
  • Minimal, calm interface — no analytics, no tracking

Plans

Ameno Pro unlocks scheduling, app-pause, and the full prayer rhythm. Available as a monthly or annual subscription, or a one-time lifetime purchase. Annual includes a 7-day free trial for eligible Apple IDs.
