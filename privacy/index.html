Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

Kinuvy (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Kinuvy is designed to minimize data collection.

Pet information (such as names, events, and logs) is stored locally on your device.

Kinuvy does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information.

Analytics

Kinuvy does not use analytics or tracking tools.

All your pet data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your Control

You have full control of your data. You can delete app data at any time by removing the app.

Medical Disclaimer
